LootScout

Steam Deal Finder & Price Comparison Extension

What is LootScout?

LootScout is a browser extension that helps Steam users find the best deals on games. It compares current Steam prices with historical data and rates deals using an 8-tier rarity system, making it easy to identify truly exceptional offers.

Key Features

Real-time Price Comparison

Compare Steam prices with current deals across multiple platforms

8-Tier Rarity System

Deal quality assessment from common to legendary based on historical data

Historical Price Tracking

Track price trends and identify the best times to buy

Multi-Region Support

Local currency display and regional pricing data

Privacy & Data

LootScout respects your privacy. The extension only accesses Steam game pages to provide price comparisons and stores minimal data locally for performance optimization.

No personal information is collected or transmitted. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why don't I see updated sale prices immediately?
A: Price data is collected from the GG.deals API, and sales are not updated the instant they go live. There may be a delay of a few minutes to hours depending on when the API data is refreshed.
Q: How does the rarity system work?
A: The 8-tier rarity system rates deals based on historical price data. Higher tiers like "Legendary" or "Mythic" indicate exceptionally good deals that are rare to find.
Q: What data does LootScout collect?
A: LootScout only accesses Steam game pages to provide price comparisons and stores minimal data locally for performance. No personal information is collected or transmitted to external servers.
Q: Why do I need to add an API key?
A: Adding your own GG.deals API key reduces rate limits and provides more reliable access to pricing data. It's optional but recommended for heavy users.
